As the Cardano community continues to grow its inevitable that some projects will go under the radar. The Cardano Watch was created to aggregate Cardano project information in one place in hopes that developers can get their projects some recognition and investors won't miss an opportunity.

Currently we are listing ISPOs and other projects that have utility. We are always looking to add more, so if you are a developer or you know of a project not listed here please send us an email at thecardanowatch@gmail.com We would be happy to list your project!
